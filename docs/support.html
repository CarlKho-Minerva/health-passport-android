Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

Health Passport requires Android 8.1 or later. For full AI capabilities (NPU acceleration), a Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 or newer processor is recommended. The app works on all Android devices in mock/demo mode.

How much storage do the AI models need?

AI models range from 50MB (PaddleOCR) to ~2.5GB (OmniNeural-4B VLM). You choose which models to download. The app works without models in demo mode.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app crashes when loading models on my device

If you have a non-Snapdragon device (Google Pixel, Samsung Exynos, MediaTek), NPU models will not work. Use the GGUF CPU fallback models instead, or try mock/demo mode.

Can I export my health records?

Your health records are stored as standard Markdown files in the app's internal storage. You can browse and edit them in the app. Export functionality is planned for a future update.
